# food-sharing-inclusion-framework
ICAN Framework – Inclusive Clustered Agri-Food Networks This repository contains research artefacts developed as part of an MSc dissertation at the University of Galway (2024–2025).
The materials support the analysis of inclusion, equity, and social sustainability within Food Sharing Initiatives (FSIs), with a focus on regional and rural–urban food systems.
Contents include:
– A 15-indicator inclusion framework (ICAN)
– Indicator definitions and domains
– Data set
These materials form the foundation for ongoing doctoral research examining inclusion and assemblage in regional food systems.
Author: Claire Davey
Status: Research framework (under development)
